About the Park

Port Moresby Nature Park is a premier tourist destination in Papua New Guinea. It combines botanical gardens, wildlife exhibits, and cultural experiences. Visitors can enjoy scenic walks, family-friendly activities, and educational programs focused on conservation and PNG’s rich biodiversity.

Main Attractions

Wildlife Exhibits

See Papua New Guinea's unique wildlife including Birds of Paradise, cassowaries, tree-kangaroos, and reptiles in naturalistic settings.

Botanical Gardens

Walk through tropical gardens and rainforest paths featuring the native flora of PNG, perfect for education and relaxation.

Family Activities

Enjoy playgrounds, picnic areas, guided tours, and interactive educational programs suitable for all ages.
